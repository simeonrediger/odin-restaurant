Claw & Crown

Come... Feast... Bask in my glory...

This restaurant is overseen by a regal feline whose presence is felt in every detail, from the velvet seating to the meticulous service. Guests dine under the watchful gaze of their cat overlord, where indulgence and obedience are equally expected. It is a place where elegance and authority converge, with each meal served as both offering and reward.
